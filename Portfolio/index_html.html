About me

Creative storyteller.
Strategic thinker.

I'm Aishwarya Sivakumar, but you can call me Aishu (pronounced EyeShoe). A 24-year-old creative storyteller, marketer, and full-time matcha and dessert enthusiast currently calling Boston, MA home. The winters are brutal. But Dunkin makes it worth it.

I'm a master's student at Northeastern University pursuing Digital Media, where I've had the privilege of working with real clients, shipping real campaigns, and learning what it actually takes to build a brand from the inside out.

My work spans brand marketing, integrated marketing communications, SEO, GEO, paid social, and digital strategy. Every project is a story. I make sure it's a good one.

During my free time you'll find me dancing, cooking something ambitious from a cooking show or an Instagram reel, or pointing my camera at something beautiful. I'm from Chennai, India, born and raised, but Boston has slowly, stubbornly, completely won me over.

I believe the best marketing respects the audience's intelligence. That's the standard I hold my own work to.

I'm genuinely curious and passionate about all things marketing. As an enthusiastic, driven business student I'm always open to new experiences, opportunities, and challenges. I love learning through mentorship programs, articles, videos, and the many inspiring people I follow on LinkedIn and social media. I enjoy attending events, workshops, and conferences where I get to be in a room with driven, diverse individuals and be challenged at case competitions that push me to think differently.

Curious by nature. Driven by purpose. Fuelled by matcha.
