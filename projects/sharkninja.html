02 · Email Marketing

2025 NPD post-launch emailer

Problem

We needed to drive awareness for our 2025 NPD through a post-launch emailer while navigating late legal updates that restricted validated claims, creating messaging challenges under tight timelines.

Solution

I developed a revised messaging brief and collaborated cross-functionally to execute it. To address legal constraints, we used a media-first approach: icons, GIFs, and visual cues to communicate key features without unverified claims, ensuring an impactful and on-time launch.

What made this hard

Late-stage legal changes

Approved claims came back restricted days before launch, requiring a full messaging pivot with no timeline extension.

Cross-functional alignment under pressure

Legal, brand, creative, and email teams all had to agree on the revised approach simultaneously.

Making visual-only claims feel premium

The pivot to icons and GIFs could have felt like a downgrade. The brief ensured the visual language elevated the product.

03 · Amazon Gallery and A+ Content

Reclaiming the official listing

Problem

Third-party sellers were dominating sales of our steam replacement pads on Amazon. Despite Shark being the genuine manufacturer, unofficial listings were outranking the official product.

Solution

I studied competitor listings to pinpoint what earned customer trust, then crafted creative and strategic messaging to restore confidence. By emphasizing Shark Genuine badges and a clean visual hierarchy, I made the information clear, credible, and impactful.

Competitive analysis framework

Missing:Genuine badge prominence. Buyers didn't know which listing was official.
Missing:Compatibility clearly stated. "Works with S1000 and S2000 series" reduces return rates.
Missing:Pad type differentiation. Scrub vs. microfiber use-case framing was absent.
Added:Clean hero shot + genuine badging + compatibility statement + use-case feature images.

04 · CGI Creative Brief

No photoshoot? No problem.

Problem

Four new attachments launched for TikTok Shop and Amazon with limited existing assets. Budget constraints prevented a new photoshoot.

Solution

We leveraged CGI as the primary visual strategy. I developed a creative brief informed by competitive analysis. We enhanced CGI visuals with realistic elements: dirt, grime, spills, and steam effects to demonstrate performance. We used brighter, modern color accents to align with TikTok Shop aesthetics while maintaining brand trust.

What the brief had to solve

Credibility without real photography

The brief specified adding realistic grime, steam wisps, and wet floor reflections to every hero shot.

TikTok vs. Amazon visual language

Two distinct color and composition treatments specified for each platform.

Four attachments, one cohesive story

Each attachment needed its own use-case visual but had to feel like part of the same system.
